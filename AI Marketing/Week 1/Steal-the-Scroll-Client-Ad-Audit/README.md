# Steal the Scroll: Client Ad Audit

**Track:** AI Marketing · **Week:** 1 · **Workload:** 3h · **Status:** Submitted

## Task
Pick a real FlyRank client, pull their live Meta and Google ads, compare against
2–3 direct competitors, and identify what's generic versus what's actually
working — then draft fresh angles for each platform.

## Approach
- Client: **I'm The Chef Too** (STEM-focused kids' baking subscription box).
  Competitors: **Raddish Kids** (adjacent, family-bonding angle) and
  **Baketivity** (closest direct rival).
- Pulled active ads from the Meta Ad Library and Google Ads Transparency
  Center — no ad account or spend needed.
- Logged each ad's hook, offer, visual style, and CTA, then used Claude to
  spot recycled patterns across the category and draft stand-out
  recommendations for Meta/TikTok (interrupt) vs. Google (intent).
